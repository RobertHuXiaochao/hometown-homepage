The three must-visit natural scenic spots in Qujing

Colorful Sand Forest

The colorful sand condenses into sand columns and reflects different tones due to different weather and light, making it extremely rare.

Jiulong Waterfall Group

These waterfalls are connected by beaches and pools, and change into different characteristic landscapes with the change of seasons.

Canola Flower Sea

When the golden yellow canola flowers bloom in spring, they present a golden sea of flowers. It is an ideal paradise for spring outings, and photography.
